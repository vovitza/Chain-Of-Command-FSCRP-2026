HIGH RANK GUIDE

Overview

The High Rank Team in Florida State Roleplay (FSCRP) is responsible for maintaining structure, enforcing rules, overseeing staff, and ensuring the community operates professionally. As a High Rank, you are expected to lead by example, make fair decisions, and contribute to the growth of the server.

High Rank Positions

  • Senior Manager
  • Manager
  • Staff Supervisor Lead
  • Staff Supervisor
  • Internal Affairs Supervisor
  • Internal Affairs Agent

These roles hold authority over Lower Ranking Staff.

Core Responsibilities

1. Leadership

  • Set a professional example at all times
  • Guide and mentor lower-ranking staff
  • Maintain activity and engagement within the team

2. Administration

  • Oversee daily server operations
  • Approve major decisions (simple changes, partnerships, etc.)

3. Moderation Oversight

  • Ensure rules are enforced properly
  • Review staff actions (warnings, bans, etc.)
  • Handle appeals and escalated situations

4. Staff Management

  • Host trainings and evaluations
  • Promote, demote, or discipline staff when necessary
  • Monitor staff performance and activity

5. Community Relations

  • Maintain a positive environment
  • Address community concerns professionally
  • Represent the server in a respectful manner

Expectations

  • Professionalism: Always remain calm, respectful, and unbiased
  • Activity: Be consistently active and available
  • Decision Making: Make fair and logical decisions
  • Confidentiality: Do not leak staff or administrative information
  • Accountability: Take responsibility for your actions and decisions

Permissions & Authority

High Ranks have the ability to:

  • Issue staff punishments
  • Approve or deny applications
  • Override lower staff decisions when necessary
  • Make announcements
Note: Abuse of power will result in disciplinary action.

Decision Guidelines

When Making Decisions, High Ranks Should:

  • Review All Evidence Carefully
  • Remain Unbiased
  • Consult Other High Ranks If Needed
  • Follow Server Policies Strictly
  • Document All Major Issues/Actions

Training

All of the high ranks must host 2 to 3 training sessions a week, this is to ensure that all high ranks are hosting training. Failure to meet the standards of hosting training will result in a warning, if this is continuous it will cause a demotion.

Promotions and Infractions

  • When promoting someone you must ensure they have a quota and they are truly active. Please make sure to be have fairness when promoting someone and please be unbiased of when you are promoting someone.
  • When you infract someone you must have a valid reason for the infraction. You must make every infraction appealable depending on the severity. Please be fair and unbiased with the infraction. Making any false infraction will result in an infraction.
  • When issuing a promotion, make sure to check out the Chain Of Command to be fully sure what rank to promote the respective staff member to.

Communication

  • Communicate Effectively With Other Staff Members, Low Rank Or High Rank.
  • Keep Staff Informed Of Any Form Of Changes.
  • Use Proper Channels To Communicate With Others, No Side-Chatting.
Note: Side-Chatting Is Not Permitted, And If Caught You Will Face Punishment.

Prohibited Actions

High Ranks Are NOT Allowed To:

  • Abuse Their Authority
  • Show Favoritism or Bias Towards Anyone
  • Leak Confidential Information
  • Disrespect Community Members Or Staff Especially Senior High Ranks
  • Make Major Decisions Without Consulting Senior High Ranks

Tips For Success

  • Stay Organized And Active
  • Be Approachable But Authoritative
  • Handle Situations Calmy
  • Always Prioritize The Server’s Best Interests
  • Continuously Look For Ways To Improve Performance

Conclusion

Being A High Rank In Florida State Roleplay Is A Position Of Trust And Responsibility. Your Leadership Directly Impacts The Success And Reputation Of The Server. Uphold Standards, Be a Role Model For Low Ranks, And Maintain A Fair And Enjoyable Environment For All Members.
